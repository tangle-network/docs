## Restaking

Tangle provides permissionless, multi-asset restaking for blueprints. Any supported asset (native or ERC-20, including bridged assets) can be deposited into the on-chain restaking system and delegated to operators.

Restaked assets act as security collateral for blueprint services requested on-demand by customers. Restakers earn incentives and service fee revenue, and accept slashing risk for the blueprints they choose to be exposed to.

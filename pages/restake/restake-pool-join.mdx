# Joining as an Operator on Tangle Network

## Introduction

Operators play a critical role in the liquid staking ecosystem on Tangle Network. As an operator, you provide the necessary services that maintain the integrity and security of staking pools, while also having the opportunity to earn rewards. This document provides an overview of how to join as an operator and the key steps involved.

## Requirements to Become an Operator

Before joining as an operator, there are several requirements that must be fulfilled:

1. **Staking a Bond:** To become an operator, you need to provide a stake or bond to the staking pool. This bond ensures that you are committed to maintaining the network's integrity.
2. **Whitelisted Blueprint:** To provide certain services, operators must support blueprints that have been whitelisted for rewards. These blueprints define the specifications for the services operators provide.

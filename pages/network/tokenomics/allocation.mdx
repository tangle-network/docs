## Vesting and Lockups

Allocations can mix unlocked and vested balances. Each allocation defines:

- **Unlock percentage** at claim or distribution time.
- **Cliff duration** before vesting starts.
- **Linear vesting period** once the cliff ends.

For legacy allocations and migration details, see [TNT Migration and Claims](/network/claim-airdrop).
